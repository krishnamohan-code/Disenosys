Career Opportunities for Automotive Electrical Wiring Harness Design Engineers!!!

  • Nishtha Tiwari
  • 26 Aug 2020

Major Automotive OEMs are looking for electrical wiring harness design engineers to minimize complexity, weight and cost. The evolution of cars, especially into electric vehicles, has opened all the doors for this particular specialisation. The industry hasn't changed much when it comes to this but is aspiring to shift to a neater and simpler handling of wire harness inside their vehicle components.

People do not look at this side much but it is a demanding field with the changing scenario. The rise of the demand of EVs and HEVs also raises concerns about the safety of the occupant when he is inside an electrified vehicle. This has added to the growth of the market. Passenger cars are going to hold a significant share of this growth.

"Asia Pacific is likely to witness the maximum demand for wire harnesses from 2019 to 2027 followed by North America and Europe. The Asian countries including China and India are experiencing a significant growth in demand for wire harness due to increasing application of wire harness in the exponentially expanding automotive sector.", writes transparencymarketresearch.com.





















The design and manufacturing of the automotive electrical wire harness depends on its purpose. The right kind of material for the right kind of job with fitting design specifications is much needed to proceed. This requires a keen understanding of the design along with the various functions different car components perform today. The designer should be able to relate to customer's demands and sensibilities if it comes to customised cars.

Government is already spending a lot in the automotive sector, like any other design engineer, electrical wiring harness designers are needed in this wave of electrification of vehicles.

Currently there are more than 500 active jobs in India alone for this job profile. They require certain skills which are as follows:-

1. Fabrication, Wiring Harness, Panels, Program Management, Calibration, Quality Control, etc.

2. Ability to assemble, test and validate wire harnesses as per customer requirements.

3. Cable/wire harness drawings

4. Demonstrated experience and ability to read and interpret.

5. Familiar with wire harnessing standards, concepts, practices & procedures in the electrical field.

6. Ability to work independently with little supervision or in a team environment.

7. Experience with using electrical / wiring tools preferred.

8. Knowledge of Schematics and specifications.

9. Prior experience with assembling wire-harnesses for military ground, air or sea vehicles is an advantage.

10. Recording problems, rework details and highlighting any improvements necessary.

11. Working knowledge of CATIA like design software.
