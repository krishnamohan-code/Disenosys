Carrer Opportunities for CAE engineers!!

  • Nishtha Tiwari
  • 26 Aug 2020

"A CAE program is a mathematical model written in a programming language using a set of algorithms that define the manufacturing processes. The process starts by first defining the analysis of the mathematical phenomenon. Next, the equations have to be defined. Finally, a model of physical configuration is created. This model may consist of 2-D or 3-D figures/shapes/curves/surfaces. This model is then applied to an actual production mechanism to design and develop the product."

According to the report, the Computer-Aided Engineering Market is estimated for USD 4665.7 Million in 2019 and is anticipated to reach USD 9747.9 Million by 2026, growing at a CAGR of about 11.10% between 2020 and 2026.


















The impact of COVID-19 around the world has slowed down things. But despite this, the market continues to grow. Any Mechanical /Automobile engineering graduate can pursue his carrier in CAE. CAE tools are widely used in the automotive industry. They are dependable, they increase safety, durability and comfort of the vehicles. The increasing EV market is nothing but golden opportunity for talented CAE engineers.

Following are the skills that CAE engineers need to have:-

  • Hypermesh, Ansys, modelling, geometry, Analysis, Stress Analysis, Nastran, Computational Mechanics, LS-DYNA, Structural Dynamics, MSC Patran, Solid Mechanics knowledge.
  • Hands-on experience in integrating the Full Vehicle, Seating system, Interiors and Exterior trims.
  • Must know FMVSS / European standards.
  • Excellent understanding of materials and manufacturing process (plastics, sheet metal forming, and die casting)
  • Excellent hands-on experience in FEA analysis of metal and plastics components.
  • Ability to understand the physical problem, translate it to an FEA model, and interpreting CAE analysis outcomes to provide design recommendations and solutions.
  • Prior experience in technical review of the team's work to ensure quality and on-time delivery. That is for experienced engineers. The same industry experience from the seasoned industry experts can be obtained at Disenosys where you can apply for the internship after completing your course in CAE.
  • Can convert physical/field problem into Simulation problem (Boundary Condition definition) independently.
  • Perform the Structural simulations, analyze the results, prepare a proper report with appropriate conclusions and suggestions to improve the product performance
  • Good in Result interpretation, may need help in design solutions.
  • Good communication skills. Strong engineering fundamentals.
