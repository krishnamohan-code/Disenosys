Carrer Opportunities as an Automotive BIW Engineer

  • Nishtha Tiwari
  • 26 Aug 2020

The passion for cars is not going anywhere soon. We have still not reached as a community to the extent of overcoming the desire to own, flaunt and drive stylish, beautiful, fast luxury cars. With major OEMs slowly recovering from the lockdown due to pandemic, there is a fair and strong chance of growth in sales of cars. A pool of job opportunities is waiting for Automotive BIW (Body in White) engineers in the automobile sector. Many brands are considering shifting their supply chains out of China to low-cost Asian countries. India ranks 18th is Automation Readiness Index and the central government is speeding up the process to establish up Industry 4.0 Excellence in India. It is taking several steps to launch India as the next manufacturing hub.

The need for BIW Engineers:-

With the cut-throat competition about to start in a few months from now, better design, strength and comfort in affordable price range is going to be the 'holy motto' of the automotive giants in India. The customers in India as always are going to expect more in less and the customers around the world will not settle for less beautiful or slower cars, they never have. The country's manufacturing and automotive design industries have to innovate, recreate and experiment more than they have done before the economic war with China. Being the 4th largest automotive industry in the world, nothing but quality and durability will be expected from Indian engineers in the post-COVID world trending with boycott China.

TATA, Mahindra, Nissan, Ford and many more local companies and startups continue to hire BIW design engineers. Their survival depends on quality, originality and talent. Hivec Rhino Technologies Pvt. Ltd., Creative Synergy Consulting India Pvt. Ltd., MorgenTEC, Vipsa Talent Solutions Pvt. Ltd. are among the many companies welcoming automotive design engineers with open hands provided they are equipped with talent and skill.

Skills they demand in general:-


  • designing BIW fixtures, etc.
  • having detailed knowledge of assembly and manufacturing process.
  • software skills like Unigraphics, CATIA, NX, etc. that can be used for 2D detailing and 3D finish.
  • capability to design sheet metal components
  • team handling experience
  • communication skills
  • out of the box thinking, innovative and original ideas always help climb the success ladder.

    • The salary of a BIW design engineer varies from 3 LPA to 10 LPA depending on his position, experience and the company that hires him. It increases eventually once he starts leading the teams and heading new projects. Any successful design will give you credit as you deserve it.

    A career as an automotive BIW design engineer is more fulfilling compared to other job roles in the automotive sector. It is almost like the brain and soul behind the final beautiful product in the market. The comfort, safety and style of the car is his team's contribution. We are never going to stop loving cars even when we switch to cleaner EVs and HEVs in future. Government easing out the policies for electrification of vehicles and its development, retrofitting, is all about new job opportunities to ambitious and talented design engineers.

    Automotive BIW market is predicted to reach USD 90.5 billion by 2027 from an estimated value of USD 77.0 billion in 2019. Rising vehicle production of passenger cars, EVs, small, medium and heavy commercial vehicles along with a reduction in the vehicle weight to meet the fuel conservation and emission standards are supposed to encourage the business. Asian countries are the winners and after the decision to end the Chinese economic hegemony, India is set to replace it.

    Presently there are 3000 BIW engineering job vacancies in India and 2342 abroad India even after lockdown restrictions. The situation is going to improve remarkably and demand is going to be on the rise after manufacturing plants and assembly lines are back into action.

    When it comes to electric vehicles production, Germany is going to lead the global market because of its advanced technology and manufacturing plants. In July 2020 alone 133 job openings for BIW engineers was offered by Germany welcoming people from across the borders. The sector is going to see a great push in the coming months when the workplace opens in normal conditions.

    The craze for racing cars and events is going to be back in the show in the post-COVID world. JA Motorsport like companies have given the passion for car racing to Indians long back and it has not diminished since then. It is expected to grow with the over-all development in ‘Make in India, Do It in India’ vision. The job opportunities are not limited to India but cross all the borders for talented people. Companies are ready to overcome all the limits to give better and best cars to the world. This dream is incomplete without BIW engineers. So don't wait, skill- up, dream and design and succeed.
