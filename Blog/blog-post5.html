JOB MARKET FOR MECHANICAL DESIGN ENGINEERS

  • Amreen Rushda
  • 26 Aug 2020

Remember the first day after your +2, when you decided to choose mechanical engineering as your career & give your passion for designing a new outlook. But, have you ever thought of jobs and placements? Will the college be able to help you with getting ’you’ your dream job? Will you be able to develop the necessary skills required to shine out in the market? Some would say ‘yes’ and some would say ‘No’ because we all walk in different shoes.

Mechanical Design Engineers are always in demand as it is one of the evergreen fields. Just think of it like this, will you stop travelling in the car? Will the companies stop manufacturing and designing automobiles? The answer is ‘No’.

To design automobiles, companies need brilliant talents thus, making the job market ever-growing. All you need is the right skill set and the right attitude. As said by Elbert Hubbard-

“Work to become, not to acquire”.

Job Opening & Salary range for Mechanical Engineers:

  • Research of the bureau of labor statistics projects 4.1% employment growth for design engineers between 2018 to 2028 and an estimated 12,800 job openings would be there.
  • Mechanical design engineers made a median salary of $87,370 in 2018. The best-paid 25% made $110,520 that year, while the lowest-paid 25% made $ 69,410.
  • There is always a demand for skilled engineers. All you need to be is a smart player rather than being dumb and following the crowd. So many employers who want ‘you’, who are waiting for talents like ‘you’. Never leave the ray of hope. As per sources, there has been a circulation of lots of false news and myth about mechanical design industry jobs.
    As mentioned by Walt Disney-
    •   

    “If you can dream it, you can do it”.

    Bursting your Myths

  • Just have faith in yourself and believe in the practicality of the real world. One will be in demand if one is demanding of oneself. In addition to this, statistical reports suggest that mechanical design engineers are projected to grow 9% from 2016 to 2026, as fast as the average of all occupations. These kinds of jobs are not only stable but also profitable. If you run your eyes around, you will find everything is related to this field. All you need is to have an eye for it. This field is growing more & innovations are what make this field more beautiful and vaster.

  • According to the reports of workable.com, Engineering jobs take the longest time to fill in comparison to other job fields where the average fill time is 47.6 days, jobs within engineering takes 60-62 days to fill. The current rate of unemployment in the manufacturing & designing industry is 3.3%. It is believed that Gen Z (people born within 1995-2010) are more interested in the jobs of the software industry rather than ME. As per the recent article of glassdoor.com, only 2% of Gen Z applied for the job of mechanical design engineer, while 19% applied for software industry jobs. This shows that there is indeed talent scarcity, not unemployment.

  • Our minds are diverted in such a way that we are made to believe in false news rather than analyzing things and focusing more on a realistic approach. Isn’t it great that you would be able to design automobiles? Wow!!! Sounds so interesting. We won’t be restricted to cubicles. We can be ’technical artists’ designing in virtual spaces or even on the job site.

  • Kelly Services, an outsourcing & consulting services, has predicted an overall increase of 11% for engineers. They concluded that nearly 2,49,000 jobs will become available by 2023. The world Economic Forum also suggested an increased growth rate of mechanical design engineer’s jobs. All we need is that engineers coming out of college must be guided & molded into something better to emerge out as shining stars in their respective fields. Generally, design engineers are full-time employees. The education level, relevant experience, the size, type & location are a few characteristics that define the average salaries. Your zeal, enthusiasm, and your complex problem-solving skills are what make you a design engineer at heart. You might not believe it but indeed it is true to the fact that to our surprise the hiring of mechanical engineers takes the longest as compared to their counterparts.


    • The Right Approach

    The reason being the scarcity of the right talents as they lack the skills what the employer demands. Wouldn’t it be so interesting if we focus on enhancing our skill set rather than blaming the job market? It is mainly centralized towards how we think.

    If you look around there are millions of opportunities waiting for you. All you need is proper guidance at the right time. When I talk to people, all they blame is the industry but no ‘stop’ we might be wrong as well. We need to stop and analyze ourselves & I can assure you with the right knowledge and the right skill set you can get your dream job at ease. As it is said, “We become what we aspire” hence, “think big, dream big”.
