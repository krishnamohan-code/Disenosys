  • Automotive Electrical Wiring Harness Design-

    • which is also known as cable harnessing.Wiring harness is an assembly of wires that helps transmit electric signals. These wires are clipped together by laces, tape, etc. The use of this course is almost in every field but the course is designed in such a way that it gives you specialisation for the automotive sector.


  • Automotive Seating Design-

    • Comfort is an attribute that today's consumers demand more and more. The seat has an important role to play in fulfilling these expectations. For occupant's comfort and health, good seat design should be applied by considering sitting postures. Automotive seats provide support, protection and comfort to the occupant. Anyone who specialises in meeting these demands remains in high demand in the industry.





















  • Automotive plastic trims-Interiors and Exteriors-

    • The design of the automotive interior and exterior plastic components is called trims. It is done in CATIA GSD Workbench. Many Major automotive design companies have separate interior and exterior design studios lacking interaction and communication. The divide has created dispersion between the two departments, leading to a design theme that does not follow throughout the whole vehicle. As a result, the best looking vehicle gets compromised in the production of the vehicle. Through this program, Disenosys quips our students both in interior and exterior trims to fill this gap and prepare them for upcoming automotive trends. the projects, pillar design and bumper design processes are designed by industry experts to give students industrial exposure.

  • Geometric dimensioning and tolerance Foundation/Expert level-

    • It is a system for defining and communicating engineering tolerances. It uses a symbolic language on engineering drawings and computer-generated 3D solid models and explicitly describe nominal geometry and its allowable variation. If you take this course you will know about the importance of tolerancing, manufacturing allowances, and various fits and functions. You will be able to perform root cause analysis using actual measured data and statistical analysis.

    There are one foundation level course and one in the expert domain that trains you for the industry.


  • CAE Career Choice Program-

    • CAE tools are widely used in the automotive industry. Their use has enabled the automakers to reduce product development cost and time while improving safety. The predictive capability of CAE tools has progressed to the point where much of the design verification is now done using computer simulations. CAE dependability is based upon all proper assumptions as inputs. If you take up this course, after completion you will gain insights on how to evaluate the design. You will be able to study the components, analyze and interpret the results.

  • Automotive Body in White master course-

    • The automotive body in white component has seen a significant growth rate in the past few years owing to increasing vehicle production worldwide.

    Despite disruptions in the automotive sector such as the evolution of electric/hybrid vehicle, development of driverless and connected vehicles, BIW components is invariable segment of the automotive sector and therefore associated as the major business segment in the overall automotive industry. The soul and brain behind the final beautiful product on roads and tracks are the BIWQ engineers. If you take up this course you will be not only be equipped with all the necessary training from industry experts but also can kick start your career in the automotive sector with a satisfactory salary package.
